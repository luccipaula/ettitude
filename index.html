About

Hi I'm Lucci, a front end developer, graphic designer and artist from Southern California. I design clean, functional and elegant solutions in the form of websites, web apps and digital graphics. Currently I work remotely as a web developer and graphic designer for a British music production YouTube channel with over 150,000 subscribers.

The artistic aspects of web development have always appealed to me most; I really enjoy making things look cool. Since experiencing how outdated college programming courses are here, I've been enthusiastically upgrading my skills with freeCodeCamp, Udemy courses and a few awesome clients. I also have a knack for adapting quickly and learning new things; some of my teachers would fondly refer to me as a sponge for knowledge. I’m eager to challenge myself in a company environment and to learn how to do so much more with design and ecommerce.

I’ve been planning to relocate to Los Angeles for a while now—ever since my first visit a few years ago I’ve loved the place. While exploring the Built In LA site, I found your Front End Developer & Graphic Designer listing and I truly believe I would be a perfect fit for the position. I am incredibly passionate about sustainability and a big proponent of shopping ethically. All in all, I really wish to further your goal of delivering beautiful, eco-friendly products and would be delighted to grow with your brand.
